About Rajkiya Madhya Vidyalaya, Araria

Rajkiya Madhya Vidyalaya in Araria, Bihar, is a government-run middle school. The school is located at V Market, 46, Station Rd, Rahika Tola, PHED Colony, Araria, Bihar. It is a part of the government education system in the Araria district.

Established in the year 1964

The school currently consists of Grades from 1 to 8. Hindi is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.

Private Building

It has got 8 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. along with a separate room for Head master/Teacher.

Source of education for many!

Being a co-educational school , Rajkiya Madhya Vidyalaya provides education to the economically weaker sections of the district.

Playground & Libraries

The school has a playground. The school has a library and has 5000 books in its library. The school has no computers for teaching and learning purposes yet.
